About me

I'm Ankit and I'm a Web Designer & WordPress Developer

I am a passionate and experienced Web Designer and WordPress Developer with over 4 years of hands-on experience in building responsive, user-centric, and aesthetically engaging websites. My expertise lies in front-end development, where I leverage a deep understanding of modern web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, Bootstrap, Material UI, and the Ionic Framework to craft clean, functional, and scalable web solutions. I have a strong command of JavaScript and ReactJS, which allows me to create dynamic, interactive interfaces and seamless user experiences. In the WordPress ecosystem, I work extensively with custom themes, page builders like Elementor and WPBakery, and plugin integration, ensuring each website is tailored to meet client goals while remaining easy to manage. My design approach is heavily informed by UI/UX principles, and I am proficient in Figma for wireframing, prototyping, and designing intuitive user flows. I pay close attention to accessibility, performance optimization, and cross-browser compatibility.
